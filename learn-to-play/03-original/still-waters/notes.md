# Still Waters

**Provenance:** Original composition by Heifer Zephyr for this floor harp build.
MIT licensed — free to perform, record, and arrange.

**Designed for:** Tony Koop's floor harp (C major, lever harp).

**Range:** D4–F5. No lever changes required.

**Form:** AABB.

**Character:** Slow and meditative — think of a glassy pond at dawn.

**Practice tips:**
- Play at ♩=80 or slower. This tune rewards patience.
- The B section climbs stepwise to F5 (the highest note) — feel the gradual
  rise and bring it back down gently.
- Optional ornament for advanced players: add a rolled chord (arpeggio upward)
  on the first beat of bar 1 and bar 9. Lift fingers one by one from low to high.
- Left hand fills: root on beat 1, fifth on beat 3. When the right hand holds a
  long note, the left can add a quiet passing tone on beat 4.
