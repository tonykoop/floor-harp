# Simple Gifts

**Provenance:** Elder Joseph Brackett, Shaker community, 1848. Published in
"The Gift to Be Simple" tradition. Public domain.

**Range:** C4–E5. Entirely in C major — no lever changes needed.

**Practice tips:**
- The opening four-bar phrase C–E–G–C is an arpeggio up the chord. Feel the
  lift as you climb.
- The B section peaks on E5 — the highest note in the tune. Keep your right
  elbow elevated so your hand angle stays consistent up in the treble register.
- Right hand plays melody throughout. Left hand: hold a root-fifth (C–G) chord
  on beat 1, lift and let ring for beats 2–4.
- This tune has been arranged for countless instruments; the simplicity is the
  point. Don't add flourishes yet — master the plain melody first.
