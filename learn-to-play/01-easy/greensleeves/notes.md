# Greensleeves (C major arrangement)

**Provenance:** Traditional English, 16th century. Public domain.
This arrangement stays in C major (no F♯, no lever changes).

**Note on the arrangement:** The original is in A Dorian/Aeolian, which
requires F♯. This C-major version transposes the feel to A minor (natural)
so you can play it with all levers in flat position. The Dorian colour is
softened slightly but the melody is fully intact.

**Range:** D4–C5.

**Practice tips:**
- Count aloud: "ONE two three, ONE two three." Beat 1 gets the melody pluck;
  beats 2–3 get the left-hand bass fill (root, then fifth).
- The arching phrase shape (climbing then descending) repeats four times with
  slight variations — focus on smooth hand coordination at the peaks.
