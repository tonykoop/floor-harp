# C Major Scale — Two Hands

**Provenance:** Original exercise.

**Range:** Right hand C4–C5; Left hand C3–G3.

**Practice tips:**
- Play the right hand alone first, listening for even tone across all strings.
- Add the left hand root-fifth pattern (C then G) only when the right hand is
  steady.
- The red C strings are your landmarks — every time you land on one, you know
  where you are.
- Listen for ringing sympathetic strings: they are normal and beautiful on harp,
  but learn to damp them before each new phrase to keep the harmony clean.
